I started being interested with the guitar a year ago.

It was not a fast process, I struggled at first because my fingers get hurt by the string too fast, and I wasn't aware where to start or what to practice first.

This video was taken 6 months after I first start playing the guitar. Although I have minimal struggles, at this point, I was able to play an entire song in one sitting

My father taught me this finger stretch exercise wherein you stretch each pair of your fingers on the back of your guitar's mango in order to accomodate it to jump more easily from fret to fret, note to note, and from one scale to another

I was able to at least decently play at that point but there's still one more thing that I struggled with: singing while playing. While it is true that I can sing, and guitar, that doesn't mean that I automatically can do both at the same time as well. One thing i've realized about that is that it requires a lot of practice from both playing and singing.  You need to seperately train them at first then doing it (playing and singing) at the same time would come out naturally.
